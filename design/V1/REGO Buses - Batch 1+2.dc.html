48 hours in Alexandria: a coastal city guide
R
REGO Editorial
20 Jun 2026
Alexandria rewards the unhurried traveler. Start your morning along the Corniche as the Mediterranean breeze rolls in, then wander toward the Qaitbay Citadel where the old harbor meets the open sea.
Where to begin
Take an early REGO bus from Cairo and you'll arrive in time for fresh seafood at the fish market. Spend the afternoon at the Bibliotheca Alexandrina before sunset over the bay.
